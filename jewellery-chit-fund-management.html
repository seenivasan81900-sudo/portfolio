Project Overview

The Chit Fund Management System is a third-tier initiative introduced by Jewellery Owners to streamline the handling of Gold Chit Fund Schemes. These schemes operate in 10-month and 11-month cycles, depending on owner requirements.

Objectives

  • Simplify gold-rate updates to reduce admin time and errors.
  • Improve dashboard navigation for faster task completion.
  • Support business growth by making the system scalable and efficient.
  • Add an upgrade plan option to boost revenue opportunities.

Solutions

Gold Rate

Admin can easily update the gold rate manually with a simple, user-friendly experience.

Gold Rate

The Outstanding Customers report provides a clear overview of pending payments, helping admins quickly follow up with members using one-click Call or WhatsApp actions.

Upgrade plan section

Built-in upgrade ads create an additional revenue stream by promoting higher-tier plans directly within the admin dashboard.

How to show it (Before - After)

Before:

Updating gold rates = 3+ screens, manual form entry, easy to miss.

After:

Inline edit on dashboard, validation, and auto-save in 1 step.

TARGET USERS

  • Small and medium chit-fund businesses.
  • Admins who update gold rates frequently.
  • Owners who track financial reports and need clear insights.

Design process

My design process is similar to that of my research ui reference. After that, I develop high-fidelity designs that focus on visual hierarchy and usability. Once I gather feedback, I iterate on the designs to improve both the user experience and the interface.

Iterative Design

Version 1

In the first design (Version 1), the dashboard followed a general layout. While functional, the gold rate update section lacked visual hierarchy and didn't immediately draw user attention.

Feedback

After testing and reviewing, I realized that updating the gold rate is one of the most frequent admin tasks. It needed to be more prominent and accessible.

Version 2

In the revised design (Version 2), I repositioned and highlighted the gold rate update card. This change improved visual hierarchy , ensured the admin could quickly find and act on it, and simplified the overall flow.
