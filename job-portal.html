Project Overview

TirupurHR is a niche job portal created for Tamil Nadu's garment industry, helping job seekers, factory HR teams, and business owners connect faster.The platform also integrates HR updates, Labour Law notifications, Acts, GOs, and consultation listings, making it a complete HR ecosystem.

Challenges Identified

Disorganized Acts, Laws & Regulations

Users struggled to find relevant HR and labour compliance acts.

Lack of Legal & HR Consultation Access

Businesses often required compliance help but didn't know whom to contact.

Consistent Daily Updates on HR & Labour Laws

The platform required frequent updates on HR, Labour Acts, and Compliance News. Users needed access to both latest and archived posts—daily, monthly, and yearly—without clutter or confusion.

Design process

I used the Learn UXapproach to design quickly and validate early. User Flow

Based on research, I designed a simple and goal-driven user flow focusing on clarity and accessibility for both job seekers and job posters.

Job Seeker Flow

1. Landing Page → Sees job categories and featured updates.

2. Register / Login→ Enters name, contact, preferred role, and experience.

3. Profile Setup→ Adds skills, qualifications, and upload resume.

4. Job Search→ Uses filters like City, Job Role, Experience, Salary Range.

5. Apply Job→ One-click apply with instant confirmation.

6. Track Application→ View applied jobs in the dashboard.

7. Explore Resources→ Check Acts, HR updates, and consultations for awareness.

Job Poster Flow

1. Login→ Employer dashboard access.

2. Post Job→ Fill in title, role, qualification, and salary details.

3. Review Applicants→ Filter by skill or experience.

4. Upgrade to Premium→ unlimited job listing & download Act Law

Solutions Implemented

Before: Hard-to-find legal content
  • Acts and Labour Laws are listed but not structured cleanly
  • Users must browse long pages
  • Content is text-heavy with no category clarity
After
Disorganized Acts, Laws & Regulations

Introduced a categorical structure: Central Acts, State Acts, and International Abstracts with subcategories for clarity. Added search and filters to simplify discovery.

Before: scattered updates across community websites
  • Huge amount of HR content
  • Updates appear in forum style
  • Hard to find a single Act or GO quickly
  • No clear categories for HR, Labour Law, and GOs
After
Consistent Daily Updates on HR & Labour Laws
  • Implemented a right sidebar timeline navigation for quick access to past months and years (up to 2 years).
  • Users can instantly jump to “May 2025” or “December 2024” without scrolling long lists.
  • Older updates automatically move into the archive system, maintaining smooth performance.
Lack of Legal & HR Consultation Access

Added a Consultation Guidance section listing verified HR & law consultants with contact details — accessible under “Business Consultation Guidance.

Design Decision & Iteration Insight

Refining the design through continuous feedback and testing to enhance both usability and visual experience.

Observation
  • The banner section lacked focus on core user action (job search).
  • Job categories were not visually prioritized, creating scroll friction.
  • Real-time stats and featured listings were missing, reducing user engagement.
  • No clear differentiation between information and interaction sections.
Key Improvements

Enhanced the homepage with a focused hero search, real-time metrics, and quick job categories. Added featured job cards, clear CTAs, and a compliance section for HR updates. Improved visual hierarchy and bilingual support for a seamless, action-driven experience.

The first version lacked compactness, with excessive white space and low contrast against the violet background. The use of multiple icons distracted from the main content, making the cards feel visually heavy and unbalance

Used a four-column layout for better balance, removed icons for clearer text, reduced white space, and increased contrast for a clean and compact design with improved readability.

Final Design

Achieved a clean, structured layout with clear hierarchy, compact cards, and user-friendly navigation for a seamless job portal experience.
